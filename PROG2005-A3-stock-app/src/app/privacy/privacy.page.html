Privacy & Security Privacy Policy

This application collects and processes inventory data, including item names, quantities, prices, supplier details, and stock status, to provide inventory management functionality.

No personal user data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties. All data is processed in accordance with the university's data protection policies.

Data Security

All inventory data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections to protect against unauthorized access.

The application does not store sensitive information locally on your device.

Your Rights

You may request access to or correction of any inventory data associated with your use of this application.

For privacy concerns, please contact the project team or your university supervisor.
